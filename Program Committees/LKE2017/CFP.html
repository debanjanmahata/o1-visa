LKE 2017: 5th International Symposium on Language & Knowledge Engineering
Benemérita Universidad Autónoma de Puebla
Puebla, Mexico, November 21-24, 2017
Conference websitehttp://lke.cs.buap.mx
Submission linkhttps://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=lke2017
Abstract registration deadlineAugust 15, 2017
Submission deadlineSeptember 10, 2017
Topics: natural language processing knowledge engineering pattern recognition information processing

The 5th Language & Knowledge Engineering Symposium (LKE 2017), organized by the Faculty of Computer Science at the Benemérita Universidad Autónoma de Puebla (BUAP), is a forum for exchanging scientific results and experiences, as well as sharing new knowledge, and increasing the co-operation between research groups in language procesing, knowledge and related areas.

Submission Guidelines

All papers must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference. 

The papers should be submitted electronically before August 31st, 2017 at the easychair system: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=lke2017.

All the papers submitted to the LKE 2017 conference will be blind submitted (author’s names are not allowed) in English. All submissions will be peer reviewed by the Scientific Committee, for originality, technical content and relevance. The final acceptance will be based upon double blind peer review of the full-length paper, and based on this the final decision will be to be considered either as ORAL or POSTER presentation.

List of Topics

Submissions are welcome in the following indicative areas (non-exhaustive):

  • Natural Language Processing
  • Knowledge Engineering
  • Pattern Recognition
  • AI and Language
  • Scholarly Information Systems
  • Information Retrieval
  • Informetrics
  • Information Processing
  • Machine Learning Applied to Text Processing
  • Humanoids
  • Social Media Analytics
  • Fuzzy Systems for Text
